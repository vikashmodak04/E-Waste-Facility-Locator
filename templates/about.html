About Us

The E-Waste Facility Locator is a platform dedicated to promoting responsible e-waste disposal and recycling. Our mission is to provide individuals with easy access to information about nearby e-waste collection and recycling facilities, educate them about the environmental and health impacts of improper e-waste disposal, and incentivize responsible behavior through a rewards system.

At E-Waste Facility Locator, we believe that every effort counts in protecting our environment and preserving natural resources for future generations. By properly disposing of e-waste and recycling valuable materials, we can minimize pollution, conserve energy, and reduce the need for raw materials extraction.

Thank you for joining us in our mission to create a sustainable future for all!
